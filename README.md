# Israel AI Compliance Toolkit

**A practical compliance framework for Israeli tech companies navigating EU AI Act, GDPR, and Israeli Privacy Law.**

Built by a legal professional transitioning into AI governance. Designed for law firms advising Israeli high-tech clients on cross-border AI compliance.

---

## Why this exists

Israeli tech companies face a unique compliance challenge:
- They build and ship AI products globally
- Their primary markets (EU, US) impose strict AI and data regulations
- Israeli law is evolving rapidly to align with international standards
- Most compliance tools are built for US or EU companies — not for Israeli startups

This toolkit bridges that gap.

## Key regulatory frameworks covered

- **EU AI Act** (Regulation 2024/1689) — enforcement begins August 2026
- **GDPR** (Regulation 2016/679) — ongoing
- **Israeli Privacy Protection Law** (5741-1981, as amended 2023)
- **Israeli Privacy Protection Regulations** (Data Security, 2017)
- **NIST AI Risk Management Framework** (AI RMF 1.0)

---

## How to use this toolkit

### For law firms
Use the checklists as a structured intake tool when onboarding new AI startup clients. The Risk Classification Framework helps you identify which EU AI Act obligations apply before the client meeting ends.

### For in-house teams
Work through the GDPR-IL Checklist to identify gaps between your current data practices and what EU market entry requires.

### For compliance officers
Use the EU AI Act Checklist to prepare for the August 2026 enforcement deadline. The checklist maps each obligation to a specific article and flags which apply to your product category.
